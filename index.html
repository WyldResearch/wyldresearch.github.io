What is Mobile Mesh Networking?

Mobile Mesh Networking leverages a smart devices ability to communicate directly with other nearby devices via Bluetooth and Peer-to-Peer Wi-Fi, extending the reach of this nearby network by allowing data to multi-hop between devices, providing communication to devices not directly connected.

What is Wyld Mesh?

Wyld Mesh is a hybrid mesh networking solution that combines mobile mesh networking with traditional OTT transport mechanisms such as Wi-Fi and mobile data. Wyld Mesh allows you to embed hybrid mesh networking into your all of your applications and devices and is designed to deliver a seamless and common experience for the user whether they are online, on the mesh or off-line.

Benefits of Wyld Mesh

Efficient

Delivers information efficiently over mobile data, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth and P2P Wi-Fi, reducing contention for existing services.

Scalable & Resilient

Able to extend reach through the formation of P2P connections that are self-forming and self-healing.

Personal & Direct

More direct and personal connections to driving better engagement and reduce exposure of information over the Internet.

Cost Effective

100% software solution that can operate without the need for hardware, sim cards or data plans.

Easy to use

No need for complicated log-in procedures or configuration.

Available as a CocoaPod

Wyld Mesh is a library available as a CocoaPod that developers can quickly and easily implement it into an existing project with just 2 lines of code.

SDKs are available, one for iOS and one for Android while the Wyld Services API delivers everything you need to implement Wyld Mesh deliver real –time notifications over the mesh.
