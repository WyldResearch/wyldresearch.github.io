Mission Statement

Wyld Research was formed to explore new mesh topologies and their associated business domains and opportunities. We're working on new ways for mobile devices to interact directly and personally, and we're designing the experience to be safe, simple, intuitive and personally unique.

The Team

Members of Wyld Research met and began working together 5 years ago, and in late 2013, came together and began solving issues with using mobile devices in hostile environments and mesh networking. During this time we developed a number of Bluetooth, Device to Device, and Mesh Prototype and Proof of Concept applications. We have mature insight into benefits and possibilities of Mesh networking, across various problem domains and network topologies.
